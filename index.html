How it works

How Sprout Works

Simple, smart, and fully automated plant care.

Vision AI

Point your camera at your plant. Sprout's built-in AI instantly analyzes the leaves and soil to see if it needs a drink.

Robotics Sync

When the plant is thirsty, Sprout sends a wireless signal directly to your LEGO Spike Prime hub to activate the water pump.

Free App

Sprout is a Progressive Web App. You can download and install it right to your phone's home screen for free!

Meet the Creators

We are Group A

We are a team of Year 7 students from Greensprings School Anthony Campus.

Our goal was to solve a real-world agricultural problem by combining software (AI) with physical hardware (LEGO Robotics). Sprout is the result of our hard work!
